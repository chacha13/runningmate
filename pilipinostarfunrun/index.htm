ADOPT-A-SCHOOL is one of our projects through our umbrella program; BUILDING GREAT BRIDGES (BGB) through Education. Now our ninth year, we chose to adopt San Isidro Elementary School in Naguillan, La Union. Part of the school building was damaged by typhoon Pepeng last November 2009 leaving part of the school building abandoned. The damaged rooms used to be occupied by grades 2 and 4 pupils. The water from the behind the school overflowed half the height of the single story building. For now, grades 2 and 4 pupils share classrooms with other classes until another classroom would be built for them. Operation Damayan aim to build two classrooms and a library for 150 pupils of San Isidro Elem. School. Construction of the proposed classrooms and library is scheduled immediately in time for the opening of school year this June 2010.
